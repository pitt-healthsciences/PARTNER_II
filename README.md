# PARTNER_II
Secondary Analysis of PARTNER II Data: Risk Factors for the Development of Long-Term PTSD, Depression and Anxiety Symptoms in Surrogate Decision-Makers of Critically Ill Patients


This is the analytic plan and statistical code for secondary analysis of data from the NIH-funded PARTNER II trial. In this analysis, we explore risk factors for development of long-term PTSD, depression, and anxiety symptoms in surrogate decision-makers of critically ill patients.
